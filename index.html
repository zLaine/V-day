V-Day

  • High Concept: Play as Cupid on Valentines day and try to get the people to get together!
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will have fun matchmaking and playing around with the different couple combinations.
  • Genre: This is a first person shooter (sort of, it involves shooting and you're playing in the first person) and action game.
  • Game play: Press the left mouse button to shoot an arrow!
  • Prototype goal: Experimentation with an unmoving first person perspective game involving projectiles.
  • Features: Amusing scoring system, rather unique concept.
  • Setting/Premise: In this game you play as the Valentine's Day character Cupid, and you're doing your yearly assignment setting couples up for romance for the evening. Taking place in a park, not all the inhabitants SHOULD be set up...Humans and cats don't belong together!
  • Target Audience: Those wanting to amuse themselves about Valentine's day -- likely those without dates.
  • Play time: 30 seconds, it SHOULD have a timer.
  • Strategies: Try to hit 2 cats to get boxes of kittens for the highest points! Avoid Human/cat couples.
  • Prototype Assets: Humans made using: http://opengameart.org/content/character-rpg-sprites by Curt (cjc83486) Background made using: "250 Handdrawn Textures" design by Daniel Cook (Lostgarden.com) http://www.lostgarden.com/2006/02/250-free-handdrawn-textures.html Cats, arrow and sound by me
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Borrows from Accelerate to Pointer, and Sprite vs Tweens examples
